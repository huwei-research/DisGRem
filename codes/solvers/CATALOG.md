# Solver catalog

## DisGrem family (proposed)
- `disgrem.py` - DisGrem (uncompressed)
- `ada_disgrem.py` - AdaDisGrem (adaptive M)
- `ce_disgrem.py` - CeDisGrem (compressed)
- `ce_ada_disgrem.py` - CeAdaDisGrem (adaptive + compressed)
- `dis_greqm.py` - DisGreQm (quasi-Newton)

## Baselines
- `dis_qn.py` - DisQN (quasi-Newton)
- `diging.py` - DIGing (1st-order)
- `esom.py` - ESOM (2nd-order)
- `extra.py` - EXTRA (1st-order)
- `sonata.py` - SONATA (2nd-order)
- `network_giant.py` - NetworkGIANT (2nd-order)
- `dqm.py` - DQM (2nd-order)
